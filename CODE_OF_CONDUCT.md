# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Being respectful and constructive in discussions
- Welcoming differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting feedback

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported privately by emailing the maintainers at conduct@terrapilot.sh. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated confidentially.

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/) v2.1.
